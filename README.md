📊 Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
📌 Project Overview

The Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project built using Microsoft Power BI. It provides a comprehensive analysis of sales, profit, regional performance, and product-level insights using retail data.

The dashboard helps identify high-performing regions, profitable product categories, and sales trends, enabling better business decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales and profit across product categories
Identify top-performing regions and states
Evaluate product-level profitability
Track sales distribution geographically
Provide interactive filtering for deeper insights
📷 Dashboard Preview

📊 Key Metrics
Total Sales: 2.33M
Total Profit: 292.30K

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance.

📈 Visualizations Included
Line Chart
Sales by Category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies)
Bar Chart
Sales and Profit by Region (West, East, Central, South)
Map Visualization
State-wise profit distribution across the U.S.
Donut Chart
Sales contribution by region
Horizontal Bar Chart
Average profit by product (Top products)
Trend Chart
Sales by State/Province
Filters (Slicers)
Category
Sub-Category
💡 Key Insights
Technology category generates the highest sales, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.
The West region contributes the most to overall sales and profit.
Certain states show high sales but lower profitability, indicating cost inefficiencies.
A few products contribute significantly higher profit margins, highlighting key revenue drivers.
